FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I know if a game is authentic?

All games on PixelPort undergo professional inspection and verification before listing. We provide detailed photos and grading information for every item. If you have any doubts, our authentication team is happy to help.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for all items. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, contact us within 30 days for a full refund. Items must be returned in original condition.

How long does shipping take?

Domestic orders typically arrive within 3-7 business days. International shipping takes 7-21 business days depending on location. Tracking information is provided for all orders.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship worldwide! Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. All international orders include tracking and insurance.

Can I sell my games on PixelPort?

Absolutely! We welcome sellers who have authentic games to offer. Contact our team to become a verified seller and start listing your collection.

How does game grading work?

We use a professional grading scale from 1-10, evaluating factors like cartridge condition, label quality, and overall preservation. Each graded item includes a detailed condition report.
